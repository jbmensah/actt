Introduction & Product Description

Product: Built-in wardrobe installed in a residential bedroom.

  • Full-height MDF wardrobe
  • Hinged doors with uniform handles
  • Shelves, drawers and hanging rails
  • Overhead cabinets and side shelving

Environment: Indoor, dry residential space

Purpose: To assess workmanship, materials, finishing, usability and customer satisfaction.

Photographic Evidence

  • Exterior view (doors closed)
  • Interior layout (doors open)
  • Shelves, rails and drawers
  • Alignment and finishing quality

These images form the basis of our evaluation.

Observation Skills (5%)

  • Uniform door gaps
  • Consistent panel thickness
  • Square carcass construction
  • Neat hinge and handle placement
  • Logical internal zoning

Measurement & Cutting Accuracy

  • Even door alignment
  • Level shelving
  • Flush drawer fronts
  • Standard 18mm board thickness

Material & Structural Integrity

Material: Laminated MDF

  • Cost-effective
  • Fast production
  • Termite resistant
  • Wide colour options

Limitation: Sensitive to moisture.

Surface Finishing & Aesthetics

  • Dark coffee wood-grain laminate
  • Matte finish
  • Smooth surface
  • Good colour harmony

Durability & Craftsmanship

  • Screws for removable sections
  • Biscuit and dowel joints for strength

Expected lifespan: 5–10 years with proper care.

Functionality & Usability

  • Hanging space
  • Folded storage shelves
  • Drawers for valuables
  • Overhead compartments

Customer Feedback

  • Customer satisfied with finish
  • Product met expectations
  • Repeat business requested

Identified Weaknesses

  • Dust on open shelves
  • Non-lockable drawers
  • Moisture sensitivity

Suggested Improvements

  • Glass doors
  • Hidden lockable drawers
  • Sensor LED lighting
  • User education

Compliance with Standards

  • 18mm standardized boards
  • Industry MDF practices
  • Safe residential use
