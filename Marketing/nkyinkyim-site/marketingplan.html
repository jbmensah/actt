Marketing Plan for a Local Carpentry Service

A practical application of basic marketing concepts using multiple channels within the local community.

Practical Task

The Service

A local carpentry service offering custom interior woodworks, furniture, and installation services for residential and commercial spaces.

Custom Carpentry & Interior Installations

Target Audience

Homeowners

Individuals looking to improve or customize their living spaces.

Airbnb / Short-let Owners

Property owners seeking durable and attractive interior finishes.

Small Businesses

Retail and commercial clients needing custom fittings and displays.

Platforms / Channels Used

Website

Serves as the main reference point where customers can view services, portfolio, and make enquiries.

Social Media

Used to showcase completed projects, engage customers, and drive enquiries.

Billboard

Offline advertising to create awareness within the local community.

Type of Content

Images

Photos of completed projects used on the website and social media.

Video

Short videos showing project processes and finished work.

Text

Captions, descriptions, and contact details to guide enquiries.

Measurement Strategy

Enquiries

Tracking calls, WhatsApp messages, and website enquiries.

Engagement

Monitoring likes, comments, and shares on social media.

Customer Feedback

Reviews, referrals, and repeat enquiries from satisfied clients.

Conclusion

This marketing plan uses a combination of online and offline channels to effectively reach the target audience, showcase carpentry services, and measure success through real customer engagement and enquiries.
