Practical Marketing Assignment

Digital Marketing Plan

Promoting a local carpentry service using digital platforms

Nkyimkyim Woodworks

Bespoke carpentry and interior woodworks service offering custom furniture, cabinetry, wardrobes and installations.

Service Overview

The Service Being Promoted

Nkyimkyim Woodworks provides custom-made carpentry solutions for residential and commercial spaces. The service focuses on quality, functionality, and visual appeal.

  • Interior installations
  • Bespoke furniture
  • Wardrobes and cabinetry
Branding

Brand Identity & Trust

Consistent Visual Identity

Same logo, colours and style across all platforms.

Professional Presentation

High-quality images build credibility and trust.

Customer Confidence

Clear branding reassures potential customers.

Differentiation

Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

  • Bespoke, made-to-measure woodworks
  • Attention to detail and premium finishes
  • Proven portfolio of completed projects
Audience

Target Audience

Homeowners

Individuals seeking custom interior solutions.

Airbnb / Short-let Owners

Property owners improving guest experience.

Small Businesses

Retail and commercial interior fit-outs.

Channels

Marketing Platforms Used

  • Website – central information hub
  • Social Media – visual engagement
  • Google Business Profile – local visibility
  • Billboard – offline awareness
Content

Type of Content Created

Images

Finished projects and interiors.

Video

Process and transformation clips.

Text

Captions, descriptions and contact info.

Evidence

Visual Marketing Assets

  • Project photographs (interiors and furniture)
  • Billboard design promoting the service
  • Social media page showcasing engagement
Measurement

Measurement Strategy (KPIs)

  • Number of enquiries via website and WhatsApp
  • Social media engagement (likes, comments, followers)
  • Customer reviews on Google Business Profile
  • Repeat enquiries and referrals
Conclusion

Summary

This digital marketing plan demonstrates how a local carpentry service can use simple digital platforms, visual content and measurable outcomes to promote services effectively within the community.
