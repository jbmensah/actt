Digital Marketing Plan for a Local Carpentry Service

A simple digital-focused approach to promoting a carpentry service within the local community.

The Service

A local carpentry service providing custom furniture, interior installations, and repair works for homes and small businesses.

Custom Carpentry Services

Target Audience

Homeowners

People looking to improve or repair their homes.

Small Businesses

Retail and office spaces needing carpentry solutions.

Local Residents

Community members seeking reliable carpentry services.

Digital Platforms Used

Website

Used to display services, completed projects, and contact information for potential customers.

Social Media

Platforms such as Instagram and Facebook are used to share images and short videos of completed work.

Offline Support Channel

Billboard Advertising

Billboards are used to create awareness and direct potential customers to the business website and social media pages.

Type of Content

Images

Photos of completed carpentry projects.

Videos

Short videos showing work in progress and finished results.

Text

Short captions, service descriptions, and contact details.

Measurement Strategy

Customer Enquiries

Number of calls, messages, and online enquiries received.

Social Engagement

Likes, comments, shares, and follower growth.

Job Conversion

Number of enquiries converted into completed jobs.

Conclusion

This digital-focused marketing plan uses simple and accessible platforms to promote a local carpentry service and measure success through real customer responses and completed jobs.
